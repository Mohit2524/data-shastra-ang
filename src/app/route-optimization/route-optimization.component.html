Route Optimization - Optimus

Overview

In the fast-paced world of logistics, delivering on time while minimizing costs and maximizing vehicle utilization presents a formidable challenge. Our clients, including major industry players in e-commerce, required a robust solution to optimize routes for single pickup, multi-drop, and last-mile deliveries.

The Problem

Our clients needed to optimize their logistical operations to reduce transportation costs while adhering to stringent Service Level Agreements (SLAs) such as on-time delivery, time slot management, and accurate Estimated Time of Arrival (ETA). The goal was to achieve maximum utilization of available vehicles without compromising service quality.

Our Solution: Optimus

Optimus:

Developed in-house at DataShastra, is a sophisticated routing algorithm that leverages a farthest-first approach combined with a Greedy algorithm. It addresses complex multi-constraint problems including traffic conditions, vehicle type specifications, and delivery slot restrictions, among others.

Advanced Algorithm Application:

Incorporates mixed-integer programming to manage constraints like vehicle load capacity, permissible delivery slots, and total working hours.

Layered Optimizations:

Enhances routes with heuristic selection based on our proprietary Network Intelligence Tool, and allows for cluster-based (pincode) or route-based delivery optimizations.

Genetic Algorithm & K-Opt Route Optimization:  

Utilizes cutting-edge methods to further refine routes and road distance calculations through our flagship product, Disha, instead of traditional Haversine distance matrices.

Impact

Cost Efficiency:

Helped reduce delivery costs by up to 60%, with an average reduction of 20-30%.

Time Savings:

Planning and deployment times were cut by 30-50%, significantly enhancing operational responsiveness.

Enhanced Accuracy and Flexibility:

Provided precise ETAs for each drop point, enhancing real-time tracking and management. Features such as manual trip assignment and the ability to split or merge trips cater to daily variations in demand and vehicle availability.

Integration Capabilities:

Fully integrated with our Smart Trip Management System and equipped with API functionality for seamless integration with third-party systems.

Additional Features

Real-Time Tracking and Alerts:

Keeps logistic teams informed with real-time updates and notifications for each delivery point.

Adaptive Trip Management:

Allows manual intervention to adjust routes and operations based on local and daily logistics dynamics.

Conclusion

Optimus by DataShastra has revolutionized how our clients approach logistics challenges. By seamlessly integrating sophisticated algorithms with practical, user-friendly features, Optimus ensures that our clients stay ahead in a competitive market, delivering efficiently and reliably every time.
