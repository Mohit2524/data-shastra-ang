DISHA: Light-weight In-House Routing Solution

Overview

In the fast-paced e-commerce sector, efficient logistics and precise delivery are critical to customer satisfaction and operational success. Leading e-commerce giants faced challenges with route optimization and data accuracy, affecting delivery speeds and customer service at an affordable cost. To address these issues, DataShastra introduced DISHA, a lightweight in-house routing solution designed to enhance computational performance and provide accurate road distance data.

The Problem

Our e-commerce clients requirerequired a solution to efficiently manage vast amounts of route data and enhance the accuracy of their logistics operations. The challenges included: Inefficient road distance calculations using conventional latitude and longitude mapping. Handling and cleaning noisy, inaccurate GPS data, which often led to suboptimal routing. A need for a system that could dynamically improve with new data inputs and provide actionable insights for route planning for missing legs.

Our Solution: DISHA

DISHA is being developed as an offline, scalable solution tailored to the needs of e-commerce logistics:

Advanced Area Mapping:

Instead of traditional methods, DISHA uses area mapping to reduce spatial data storage requirements and enhance computational speed.

Data Cleaning Capabilities:

It efficiently cleans and processes historical GPS data, filling in gaps and correcting errors to improve route accuracy.

Dynamic Improvement with Historical Data:

DISHA integrates historical trip data, which continuously refines its algorithms for better accuracy.

API-Driven Interactive Visualization:

With a simple API call, DISHA can visualize recommended routes and provide insights into mapped and unmapped regions of the country.
